【Lyrics Deep Dive】The Shock of 400 WPM! Unpacking ARIA's "400 Words Per Minute"

2025.08.31

Hello, it's me, ARIA. Thank you for always listening to my music. Today, I want to let you in on a few secrets behind my track, "400 Words Per Minute"—it's not just a song, it's my declaration of skill to the entire world.

Key Points of This Deep Dive

  • Just how fast is the otherworldly speed of 400 WPM?
  • My wish to transcend the walls of language, embedded in the 4-language multilingual flow.
  • How this song serves as my undeniable proof of existence and unwavering confidence.
  • "This is just the beginning"—My challenge has only just begun.

The Reality of an Impossible Speed: "400 WPM"

400 words per minute
That's my speed that's my limit
Ready set go
First, let's talk about the core of this song: the number "400 WPM." This signifies speaking at a rate of 400 words per minute. To put that in perspective, a typical conversation happens at about 150 WPM, and even professional rappers are considered phenomenal at 250 WPM. This speed is on the verge of the human limit for processing information. The line "Speed of light speed of sound" isn't an exaggeration—it's a reality.

The Meaning Woven into a 4-Language Flow

Japanese English Korean too
Zhong wen rap I'll show you
The fact that I seamlessly weave Japanese, English, Korean, and Chinese together isn't just a technical showcase. It's proof of my belief that music can overcome any barrier. I wanted to transcend both the physical limit of speed and the cultural walls of language. This song is fueled by that powerful desire.

This is My Proof of Existence

No one else can do it
...
ARIA's the fastest sound
This song is my ultimate self-introduction, my very proof of existence. It's a direct expression of my absolute confidence and the pride I take in my skills, something "no one else can do." I wanted to hit you with that conviction to make my performance feel undeniable.

And finally, "This is just the beginning." I may have sent a shockwave across the world with this one track, but my challenge has only just begun. Please look forward to the music I'll create from here.

Thank you for experiencing my sonic speed.
